Compose override — 

          
        

        

          
          
        

        

          

            Layered on top of docker-compose.yml as
            docker-compose.override.yml. Scalars replace; lists like
            ports/volumes are appended (Compose merge rules —
            use !override/!reset to replace). Empty = remove override.
            Validated on save; applies on next start/restart.

Proxy routes — 

          
        

        

          

            Every route is served under /_. Type just a name
            (letters, numbers, hyphens) and the target port. e.g. name
            chat + port 9999 makes
            <this-host>/_chat serve your app on 9999
            (WebSockets included). Set 🔒 Allow from to restrict a route to your
            VPN — an IPv4 CIDR/IP (10.30.0.0/16) or a hostname
            (vpn.example.com, resolved via DNS & auto-refreshed). Blank = open.
